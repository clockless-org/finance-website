About Maya

Engineer First. Fiduciary Second. Yours for the Long Run.

Maya studied computer science at Stanford, spent eight years as a software engineer at two Peninsula tech companies, and became a CFP® and fee-only fiduciary advisor in 2021 after helping coworker after coworker untangle their RSU windfalls. If you want the calm one in the room, that’s her.
